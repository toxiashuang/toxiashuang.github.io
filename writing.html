{ Writing }

The thread of language is woven deep into the fibers of who Susan is. The pen and paper were synonyms for comfort and solace. In her youth, fantasy and science fiction were her late night companions. As Susan has grown and tasted more of life, many different styles have been added to her repetoire. Now crowded into her busy mind are clamorings of AP style, research papers, journalings, blogs, and many many more.
