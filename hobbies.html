Music

Susan is a popcorn listener, enjoying what people recommend her; bits and pieces of albums rather than full-on devotion to any particular artist. She enjoys singing for her praise team and church and playing guitar for her own enjoyment.

Video Games

Growing up with a younger brother, Susan was immersed in games such as Age of Mythology and Starcraft, Portal and Brawl. Zelda, Final Fantasy, and League of Legends were all among her short forays into the world of gaming, to which she sporadically returns.
